TeamWork

As a team meeting solution, TeamWork makes working with multiple different people, in multiple different locations, efficient and effective. It includes audio and/or video conferencing for up to 8 participants, though thereâ€™s no limit to the number of people who can participate in a workspace text chat.

Whether organizational meetings or meetings with outside employees, contractors or others, TeamWork can easily replace third-party conferencing solutions.

  • Audio and Video Conferencing

Audio and Video Conferencing

A TeamWork audio/video conference can accommodate up to 8 individual participants, but those participants can be from anywhere: they do NOT have to be part of your organization. Each user has full control over their own audio and video stream, meaning that they can disable their own audio or video if someone walks into their office, etc.

Take Control

Meeting organizers have total control over any TeamWork they create: they can restrict anonymous access, set permissions for file uploads, set the defaults for video and audio quality, password protect the meeting and much more.

  • Group Chat
  • File Sharing
  • Eliminate Third-Party Services

Group Chat

Group chat from WebCom Email is also available in all areas of the workspace. You can text chat with individuals across all the features - including during a video conference - the chat includes things like real-time photo previews, link previews, the ability to add and upload files and more.

File Sharing

The File Storage area of a TeamWork can be controlled by the Workspace organizer, meaning they can control who can, and can't, upload files to the meeting. However, any participant can download files as needed, even long after the meeting has ended.

Eliminate Third-Party Services

You can create teamwork and use them once, or the same workspace can be used multiple times or as a continuous meeting space. Regardless, all chat history and file uploads are stored on the server for later use or for compliance needs. However you use them, TeamWork are the ideal place to communicate and can replace services such as #Slack or Microsoft Teams, without incurring any additional expense!
